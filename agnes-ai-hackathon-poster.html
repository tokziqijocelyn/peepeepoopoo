Agnes AI Fashion Hackathon

FitCheck turns your closet into a personal AI stylist.

A mobile-first fashion assistant that understands what you own, learns what you like, and shows you what to wear or buy with Agnes AI text and image models.

Problem Statement

Style decisions are fragmented.

People own clothes they forget about, shop without context, and struggle to imagine how outfits will look on their own body before wearing or buying.

Solution

One loop: closet, swipe, shop.

FitCheck creates a digital wardrobe, generates outfit cards, learns from swipe-rights, and builds a personalized shopping feed from each user's style profile.

Unique Selling Point

AI that styles you, not a mannequin.

Agnes AI combines clothing metadata, taste signals, budget, occasions, and optional body photos to produce personal outfit and try-on previews.

Demo Magic

Tinder-style fashion discovery.

Swiping makes feedback effortless. Every like becomes a stronger signal, so recommendations feel more personal the longer the user plays.

Best Use Cases for Agnes AI

Smart Closet Uploads

Describe clothing images, detect categories, colors, tags, and style vibes automatically.

Outfit Generation

Combine wardrobe items with style profiles and recent likes to create fresh daily looks.

Virtual Try-On

Generate editorial-style visuals of outfits on the user's body reference for confidence.

Personalized Shopping

Turn questionnaire answers into realistic product ideas, previews, store badges, and prices.
